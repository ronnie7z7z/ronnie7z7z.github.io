JUST DANCE TO THE FLOW!

Let me tell you, before coming to college, I never danced for almost 7-8 years. I started it when we had to prepare performances for Salsa night and I was a part of Hip-Hop performance. That's where it all began; The love for dance. Hip-Hop and Bollywood are the dance genres I usually perform but I would love to learn comtemporary style. Maybe I'll do that when I lose some weight though! The Annual InSync Dance Show (AIDS) 2019 which was held in January gave me and many freshies an opportunity to dance in front of a huge crowd and on the convocation stage. It was simply mesmerizing!

In the campus we have clubs for almost everything, so we do for dance too. InSync is the name of Dance Club of IITB is existent from 2003. Through this club, I've made many friends and seniors in this club are awesome. We also had AIDS's trip (sounds weird, I know) which was a beautiful day indeed. Among all the clubs, InSync is the most lovely club I've seen!
